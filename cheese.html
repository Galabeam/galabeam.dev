CHEESE

There have been many great foods in the history of the world. Whether these foods are cultural, healthy, or simply amazing they hold special places in all of our hearts, and stomachs. However, there is truly no other food that can be compared to the only true best food in the world: cheese. Cheese is used in numerous foods, is made from the best of the best, and is everything you could possibly dream of.

Cheese is a key part of numerous baking and cooking related foods, such as mac n cheese, pasta, pizza, mozzarella sticks, lasagna, cheesecake, cheeseburgers, grilled cheese sandwiches, and many more popular foods. Not only this, but cheese is one of the results of milk, which is used in all baking recipes and is very good for your health, on top of tasting good. Milk is also used to make cream cheese, ice cream, smoothies, and more.

Cheese is also a fantastic way to get protein and calcium, which are critical for one's health. Eating cheese can benefit one's health massively on top of tasting so good! If one cheese doesn't fit your needs, or to you taste bad, don't worry, as there are many types of cheese! There's blue cheese, cheddar cheese, mozzarella cheese, swiss cheese, American cheese, cottage cheese, goat cheese, and many other flavors available.

Out of all the foods in the world, no matter from where, cheese is clearly on top as one of the best foods in the world. It is used in a plethora of foods on top of stemming from the even more versatile milk, and is healthy!

Overall, Cheese.
